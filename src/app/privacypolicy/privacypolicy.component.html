Privacy Policy - Book My Meal

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Book My Meal collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Such as your name, email address, and phone number when you create an account.
  • Order Information: Details about the orders you place through the Book My Meal platform.
  • Usage Information: Data about how you interact with the Book My Meal service, including pages visited and actions taken.
  • Device Information: Information about your device, such as the type of device, operating system, and browser.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve the Book My Meal service.
  • To process and fulfill your orders.
  • To communicate with you about your account and orders.
  • To analyze usage and improve the user experience.

For more details, please review our Terms and Conditions.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access or disclosure.

Changes to This Privacy Policy
